Becoming a student at AHR is now easier than even, our new registration system makes the process as easy and stress free for everyone.

The registration process consists of 3 steps :

  • Preliminary Form: general information about the applicant.
  • Main Phase: the applicant will choose course , payment type, and scholarships if any.
  • Visa Creation(international only): the applicant will receive the visa if he's international.
after these steps the applicant will be able to arrive at AHR and complete the needed procedures for dorm selection and classes registration.

before we get started, make sure we offer the course you want:

  • Undergraduate courses
  • Postgraduate courses
